## 🚨 REALITY CHECK

**Truth about "active airdrops":**

1. **Most "airdrops" are actually:**
   - Protocol rewards programs (Aave, Compound)
   - Points systems (Linea, Scroll, Blast)
   - Governance token claims (from months ago)
   - NOT traditional "free money" airdrops

2. **Traditional airdrops are rare:**
   - Major ones: 2-3 per year
   - Medium ones: 5-10 per year
   - Most are expired within 3-6 months

3. **Best approach:**
   - Focus on **claimable rewards** (DeFi protocols)
   - Track **points programs** (convert to tokens later)
   - Monitor **testnets** (future airdrops)
   - Check **portfolio aggregators** (DeBank, Zapper)
